Question : A carbohydrate that cannot be hydrolysed further to give simpler unit of polyhydroxy aldehyde or ______ is called a monosaccharide..
Answer : ketone
 
Question : A carbohydrate that cannot be hydrolysed further to give simpler unit of polyhydroxy ________ or ketone is called a monosaccharide..
Answer : aldehyde
 
Question : A carbohydrate that cannot be hydrolysed further to give simpler unit of polyhydroxy aldehyde or ketone is called a ______________..
Answer : monosaccharide
 
Question : A ____________ that cannot be hydrolysed further to give simpler unit of polyhydroxy aldehyde or ketone is called a monosaccharide..
